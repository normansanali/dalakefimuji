Seth Rollins Crowned PWIs Wrestler of the Year for 2023

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

In 2023, WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins achieved a significant accolade by being voted as the Wrestler of the Year by Pro Wrestling Illustrated. The results of the fan-voted awards for the year were unveiled in the latest edition of PWI, showcasing a remarkable year for WWE talent.

Seth Rollins emerged as the standout performer, claiming the prestigious Wrestler of the Year award, while Rhea Ripley was recognized as the Woman of the Year.

The complete list of award winners for 2023 is as follows:

  • Wrestler of the Year: Seth Rollins
  • Woman of the Year: Rhea Ripley
  • Most Popular Wrestler of the Year: Cody Rhodes
  • Faction of the Year: The Judgment Day
  • Tag-Team of the Year: FTR
  • Match of the Year: Rhea Ripley vs. Charlotte Flair
  • Inspirational: Mark Briscoe
  • Comeback of the Year: Trinity
  • Most Improved: LA Knight
  • Rookie of the Year: Sol Ruca
  • Indie Wrestler of the Year: Matt Cardona
  • Feud of the Year: Sami Zayn vs. The Bloodline
  • Most Hated of the Year: Dominik Mysterio

These awards celebrate the outstanding contributions and achievements of wrestlers in the world of professional wrestling in 2023.
