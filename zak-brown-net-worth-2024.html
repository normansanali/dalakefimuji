Early Life and Career Beginnings

Zak Brown was born in Los Angeles, California, and developed a passion for racing at a young age. His career in motorsports began as a driver, but he soon realized his talent lay in the business side of the sport. He founded Just Marketing International (JMI), which quickly became one of the most successful motorsport marketing companies globally.

Establishing JMI and Increasing Wealth

Under Brown’s leadership, JMI attracted high-profile clients such as Subway, UPS, and Verizon. The company’s success significantly contributed to Brown’s wealth, and in 2013, he sold JMI for a reported $76 million, further boosting his net worth.

Role at McLaren and Financial Growth

In 2016, Brown took on the role of Executive Director at McLaren Technology Group, and later became the CEO of McLaren Racing. His leadership has been instrumental in the team’s improved performance in Formula 1, which has undoubtedly had a positive impact on his earnings.

Other Business Ventures

Beyond JMI and McLaren, Brown has been involved in other business ventures. He co-founded the Motorsport Network, a digital platform for motorsport and automotive fans. Real Estate Investments
