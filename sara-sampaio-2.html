Sara Sampaio known as Sara Pinto Sampaio is a Portugal model. As of 2022, Sara Sampaio’s net worth is $10 million. Sara has worked with many popular fashion labels such as Vogue, Elle, Armani, etc. She was chosen as the face of the Italian fashion brand Calzedonia.
