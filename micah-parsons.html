Micah Parsons- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

Micah Parsons- Professional Career

Parsons grew up in Sunshine Park, where he used to play football. And began playing professionally while still in high school. He rushed for 1239 yards and 27 touchdowns his final year. He was a five-star recruit who finished fourth in his class. Nebraska, Georgia, Oklahoma, Ohio State, Alabama, and Penn State were all interested in him. In his freshman year after committing to Penn State on December 20th, 2017, he led his team in tackles with 82.

He finished his sophomore season with 109 tackles, 5 sacks, 3 deflected passes, and 3 caused fumbles. He was voted the 2019 Cotton Bowl Classic defensive MVP after recording 14 tackles, two sacks, and two forced fumbles. The Dallas Cowboys selected Parsons in the first round of the NFL Draft 2021 after trading down with the Philadelphia Eagles from their original pick. He is currently on the list of rookies to watch in NFL 2021, and he is expected to play his first-ever NFL game in the coming days.
