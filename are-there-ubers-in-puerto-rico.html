Is Uber reliable in Puerto Rico?

Yes, Uber is considered reliable in Puerto Rico.

Is it hard to get an Uber in Puerto Rico?

Getting an Uber in Puerto Rico is not difficult, but availability may vary outside of the capital, San Juan.

Are Ubers expensive in Puerto Rico?

Uber in Puerto Rico is generally more affordable than traditional taxis. Prices may vary depending on the distance traveled.

How do you get around in Puerto Rico?

Publicos, taxis, and buses are popular methods of transportation in Puerto Rico. Publicos are regulated vans that offer transportation between major locations.

Should you rent a car in Puerto Rico?

Renting a car in Puerto Rico is a good option if you want more independence and flexibility in your travels. However, it is not necessary if you plan to stay in the main tourist areas.

Is it safe to take Uber in San Juan Puerto Rico?

Yes, taking an Uber in San Juan, Puerto Rico is considered safe. Uber drivers undergo background checks for safety measures.

Are taxis reliable in Puerto Rico?

Taxis are generally reliable in Puerto Rico, but some may operate without meters.

Do you tip taxi drivers in Puerto Rico?

Tipping taxi drivers is common practice in Puerto Rico, with 10-15% of the total fare being appropriate.

What currency is used in Puerto Rico?

Puerto Rico is a territory of the United States and uses the U.S. dollar (USD) as its currency.

Do taxis in Puerto Rico take credit cards?

Some taxis in Puerto Rico accept credit cards, but it is best to inform the driver in advance if you plan to pay with a card.

How much is a taxi from San Juan Airport to Old San Juan?

The cost of a taxi from San Juan Airport to Old San Juan is approximately $21, with an additional $3 surcharge for travel to/from the airport.

Do taxis run 24/7 in Puerto Rico?

Taxis in Puerto Rico are available 24/7 and can be hailed on the street or called in advance.

What is the cheapest way to get around Puerto Rico?

The cheapest way to get around Puerto Rico is by using publicos, which are public minibuses that run on prescribed routes during daylight hours.

What is the best mode of transportation in Puerto Rico?

Renting a car is often considered the best mode of transportation in Puerto Rico as it provides flexibility and convenience. However, public transportation options are also available.

Does Puerto Rico use USD?

Yes, Puerto Rico uses the U.S. dollar as its official currency.

Can Uber pick up at Puerto Rico airport?

Yes, Uber drivers can pick up passengers at the airports in Puerto Rico.

Is Puerto Rico expensive as a tourist?

The average daily budget for a trip to Puerto Rico can vary, but a reasonable estimate is around $100 to $150 per day. This includes accommodations, food, transportation, and activities.

Can I get around Puerto Rico without a car?

While it is possible to get around Puerto Rico without a car, it can be challenging, especially outside of the main tourist areas. Renting a car is recommended for more convenience.

Is food in Puerto Rico cheap?

Food prices in Puerto Rico can vary, but street food and fast food options are generally inexpensive, while mid-range restaurants can be more expensive.

How much money should I bring for 5 days in Puerto Rico?

A budget of $180 per person per day should be sufficient for a 5-day trip to Puerto Rico, including accommodations, food, transportation, and activities.

Do I need cash in Puerto Rico?

While many establishments in Puerto Rico accept debit and credit cards, it is recommended to carry cash as some places may have issues with card readers. ATMs may charge a fee for cash withdrawals.

How much is a cab from San Juan Airport to cruise port?

Do taxis run 24/7 in Puerto Rico?

Yes, taxis in Puerto Rico operate 24/7 and can be hailed on the street, picked up from taxi ranks, or called in advance.
