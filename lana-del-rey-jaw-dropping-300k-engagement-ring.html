Lana Del Rey shows off $300k 'engagement ring' and it's blinding!

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Lana Del Rey has reportedly been engaged to her boyfriend, Evan Winiker, for weeks and even showed off her dazzling engagement ring earlier this month.

The 'Summertime Sadness' singer attended the Billboard Women in Music Awards where she was pictured wearing a blinding diamond ring on her engagement finger, which is said to be worth a whopping $309k! 

According to Jessica Flinn-Allen, CEO of wedding and engagement ring specialists Jessica Flinn, Lana's round brilliant cut trilogy ring has an approximately 3.00ct center stone set in platinum. 

The ring features a large, round diamond in the center, flanked by two smaller round-cut diamonds on either side, creating a symmetrical and elegant design.

Commenting on the style, Jessica Flinn-Allen said: "The three-stone style of the ring is a classic choice for engagement rings, symbolizing the past, present, and future of the couple's relationship. 

"The side stones on Lana Del Rey's engagement ring not only add to the symmetry and overall beauty of the ring, but they also serve an important purpose in enhancing the sparkle and brilliance of the center stone."

Lana has kept her relationship with the talent agency managing partner and musician extremely private, with the couple only being spotted together on a few occasions. Neither of them has confirmed an engagement. 

Their last public sighting was in March at Pappy and Harriet's restaurant in Pioneertown, Calif, where they were spotted taking photos with fans. 

Lana's love life has been a topic of interest over the years. In the past she has been linked to filmmaker Mike Hermosa, musicians Jack Donoghue and Clayton Johnson, and police officer Sean 'Sticks' Larkin of Live PD.

In 2014, she ended her relationship with Scottish musician Barrie-James O'Neill, who now goes by the stage name Nightmare Boy. The pair had been together for three years before Lana confirmed their split in an interview with Switzerland's 20 Minuten newspaper. 

At the time, Lana spoke candidly about the challenges they faced as a couple, stating: "We are currently not together. He is a wonderful person, but there are some things with which he has to deal. "I will not explain it in detail. This was hard on our relationship; I no longer felt free. We'll see how it goes."
