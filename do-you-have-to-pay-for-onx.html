Do you have to pay for onX?

August 2024 · 4 minute read

As an Ecotourism Specialist, I often get asked about various tools and apps that can enhance the outdoor experience. One app that frequently comes up in discussions is onX. Many people wonder if they have to pay for onX and what features are included in the free version. In this article, I will address these questions and provide more information about onX.

Is onX Offroad App Free?

The answer is yes! The free version of the onX Offroad App includes satellite, topo, and hybrid maps, as well as the ability to track your trips and add markups. This means you can use the app to plan your off-road adventures and navigate with confidence without having to pay a penny. However, it’s important to note that the free version does not include access to off-road trails, trail descriptions, recreation points, and the ability to download unlimited offline maps.

Can You Use onX Without Paying?

Yes, you can use onX without paying. Any new user of onX Hunt can use the app and web map for free for seven days. This trial period allows you to experience the full range of features and decide if you want to become a premium member. After the free trial, if you choose not to become a premium member, you will still be able to use onX Hunt’s basic features for free for a single state, which includes GPS tracking.

Does onX Require a Subscription?

While you can use onX without paying, there is also the option to upgrade to a premium membership. The pricing for onX Hunt is $29.99 per year for the Premium version, $44.99 per year for the Premium Two-State version, and $99.99 per year for the Elite version. These memberships unlock additional features, such as access to highlighted open and closed off-road trails, featured trails with descriptions and photos, recreation points, and unlimited offline maps. You can also start a free trial and receive premium features for seven days.

What is the Difference Between Free and Premium onX?

The free version of onX Offroad includes the ability to use satellite, topo, and hybrid maps, save one offline map, track trips, and add limited waypoints. On the other hand, the premium version includes highlighted open and closed off-road trails, featured trails with descriptions and photos, recreation points, and unlimited offline maps. If you want to have access to these additional features, upgrading to the premium version may be worth considering.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1. Can I use onX without an internet connection?
Yes, onX allows you to save maps to your phone or tablet and access full location and trail data even when you’re out of service. You can view your saved maps using your device’s internal GPS.

2. How accurate are the property lines shown on onX?
The onX team works with data sources to ensure accuracy, but it ultimately depends on the individual county and data source. Private property boundaries are generally accurate to within 5-10 feet.

3. Can onX show me where I can hunt?
Yes, onX Hunt accurately shows property information for private and public lands, including property boundaries.

4. Are there any free alternatives to onX?
There are other apps available that provide similar features, but onX is highly regarded for its accuracy and user-friendly interface.

5. How often are property owner updates made?
Private land updates are typically made every one to two years, but the accuracy ultimately depends on the county and local data sources.

6. Can I use onX for off-roading?
Yes, onX Offroad is specifically designed to help off-roaders discover and navigate trails for various vehicles, including 4x4s, UTVs, dirt bikes, and snowmobiles.

7. What other features does onX offer?
In addition to the features mentioned earlier, onX offers premium map tools, pro deals, expert resources, application research tools, and much more.

8. How many people use onX?
Today, onX has hundreds of thousands of members, and more than a million people use the free version of the app regularly. Its popularity speaks to its usefulness and reliability.

In conclusion, onX offers a free version of its app that includes essential features like satellite and topo maps, trip tracking, and markups. However, upgrading to a premium membership unlocks additional features such as access to off-road trails, highlighted routes, and unlimited offline maps. Whether you choose to use the free version or upgrade to premium, onX is a valuable tool for outdoor enthusiasts looking to enhance their adventures.
