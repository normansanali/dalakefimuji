RAPPER and celebrity boxer Blueface has been stabbed in a gym attack on Wednesday. 

The 26-year-old music star turned amateur boxer revealed on social media a video of him training in a gym in Los Angeles when he got into a physical altercation with a guy at the gym. 

According to the rapper, the stranger reportedly stabbed him with a knife after the two began arguing.

Blueface took to Instagram and informed fans that he would miss his upcoming fight due to the injury.

"I won't be able to fight [on] October 14 due to an injury. "

"[I] won't have enough time to heal. Don't say I ducked none. Bro came with a dog and a knife at 10 a.m."

The security footage on his social media account shows his trainer, David Kaminsky, trying to intervene and defuse the situation and get between the man and Blueface.

However, the man then appears to take something out of his pocket and head toward Blueface before the video cuts off.

Kaminsky told TMZ that the argument escalated quickly, and the unknown man began yelling threats at the rapper.
