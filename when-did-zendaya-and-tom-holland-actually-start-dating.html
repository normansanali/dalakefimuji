When Did Zendaya And Tom Holland Start Dating?

It all started when they played love interests in the 2016 Spider-Man movie. After filming, the two did lots of press together and shared pictures on Instagram together. They were also constantly seen together in public and Holland even met Zendaya's family for dinner in New York City in 2017. This made fans speculate that the relationship was more than the 'friendship' they were making it out to be.

They shared their first on-screen kiss in Spider-Man: Far From Home in 2019 as Peter and MJ. This fueled the fire even more that they were together because of their chemistry in the movie. In 2020, both Zendaya and Holland were rumored to be dating other people. Zendaya was spotted with Euphoria co-star Jacob Elordi and Tom Holland was seen with actress Nadia Parks.

The pair was seen together again on Tom's Instagram for Zendaya's 25th birthday. The caption to the photo led fans to believe the two were dating again.
