Calvin Ridley Career

Calvin Ridley was selected by the Atlanta Falcons in the 2018 NFL Draft. He was chosen in the first round of the draft and went on to be the 26th overall pick.

The Philadelphia Eagles were Ridley’s first opponents in the NFL.  Ridley led all rookies in receiving yards and touchdowns last season.

As a result, he was chosen to the PFWA’s All-Rookie Team. He is now only the fourth Atlanta Falcons receiver to win this award.

Ridley suffered an abdominal injury during a game against the Carolina Panthers in Week 14 of 2019. He was ruled out for the full season due to the injury.

Despite this, he concluded the season with 63 receptions for 866 yards and seven touchdowns.
