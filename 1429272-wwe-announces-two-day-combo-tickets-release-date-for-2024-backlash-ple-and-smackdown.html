WWE Announces Two-Day Combo Tickets Release Date For 2024 Backlash PLE And SmackDown

August 2024 · 1 minute read

WWE has provided an update on the upcoming ticket releases surrounding WWE Backlash.

Last month, WWE announced the date and location for their 2024 Backlash premium live event. Next May, WWE will head to Europe for Backlash, hosting the event from the LDLC Arena in Lyon-Decines, France. This will mark the first-ever WWE PLE to take place in France. Earlier today, WWE revealed that two-day combo tickets for Backlash, as well as the preceding episode of SmackDown, will be made available for purchase beginning next month.

Presales for the two-day combo tickets will begin on Wednesday, January 10 at 10 a.m. CET (4 a.m. EST) WWE fans have the opportunity to register for his exclusive presale offer by visiting: https://www.wwe.com/backlash-2024-presale. Following the conclusion of WWE’s pre-sale, general ticket sales for the two-day combo will begin Friday, January 12, at 10 a.m. CET (4 a.m. EST) through Ticketmaster France.

WWE SmackDown in France will take place on Friday, May 3, while Backlash will broadcast live the following day on Saturday, May 4. Both shows will emanate from the LDLC Arena in Lyon-Decines.

WWE also confirmed that SmackDown and Backlash Priority Pass ticket packages will be available soon through On Location. On Location provides fans with opportunities for premium seating, ringside photographs, exclusive merchandise and more.
