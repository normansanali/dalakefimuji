Natalie Lee from Love is Blind season 2 lives life to the fullest judging by IG Sat 12 February

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Thirty singletons are taking part in a social experiment as of February 11th, 2022. The participants on Love is Blind season 2 are ready to find ‘the one’. After failed dating experiences in the past, they’re willing to place their fate in the hands of the experiment which takes away all physical elements of dating and the couple’s connections are built solely on their connection and emotions from talking over 10 days in individual pods.

Preconceptions, superficiality and stereotypes are all out the window on Love is Blind. One of the stand-out cast members on season 2 is Natalie Lee. So, let’s find out more about Natalie and her partner, Shayne Jansen…

Love is Blind season 2: Meet Natalie

Natalie Lee is a 29-year-old consultant.

She wants to meet someone whose personality fits with hers and she also added that she decided she wanted to get married when she saw her grandfather caring for her grandmother with Alzheimer’s.

Speaking of her past dating experiences, Natalie said: “When I meet men on dating apps, I think they see an Asian and they stereotype me thinking I’m gonna be quiet more submissive and then they’ll meet me and I’m a bit louder, more outgoing and more career-oriented and opinionated.“

Natalie and Shayne

Natalie hit it off pretty quickly with 31-year-old real estate agent Shayne Jansen (@shaynejansen) in the pods.

They both had high energy and said that their conversations flowed well right off the bat.

However, Shayne’s slip-up confusing Natalie for another participant called Shaina threw a spanner into the works quite early on.

Despite the pods awkwardness, Shayne proposed to Natalie and the two went on their honeymoon. Many of the other people in the Love is Blind 2022 experiment said that they could see Natalie and Shayne lasting as a couple.

Meet Natalie Lee on Instagram

Natalie Lee has over 4.7k followers on Instagram @natalieminalee.

Judging by her IG page, she’s very adventurous and loves to travel. Natalie’s Instagram page sees her tagged all over the world including Seoul and Portugal.

She can also be seen skiing in Vermont and going on safaris in Tanzania. Natalie clearly lives life to the fullest and can be seen taking pics with her friends and family on the ‘gram.
