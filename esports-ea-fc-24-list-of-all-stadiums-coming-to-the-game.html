EA FC 24 is the latest installment in the acclaimed football video game series developed by EA. In this highly anticipated release, EA has gone above and beyond to create a truly immersive and captivating experience for football enthusiasts worldwide. EA even after breaking its partnership with FIFA, still owns licensing rights to more than 1000 players and numerous leagues.

EA FC 24 also includes a diverse range of stadiums that promise to elevate the gameplay to new heights. The publishers earlier announced the inclusion of about 115 licensed stadiums. The game boasts over 150 stadiums if you include generic venues like the Waldstadion and the FUT Arena, as well as various VOLTA arenas and pitches. Let’s take a look at the list of stadiums we will be getting:

All stadiums coming to EA FC 24

EA has recreated some of the most iconic and beloved football stadiums from around the globe along with using the Hypermotion V technology, Frostbite, and Playstyles in the gameplay to display their unwavering commitment to authenticity. The game has 115 licensed stadiums which is a big increase from FIFA 23’s 105 stadiums. The fan-favorite Camp Nou returns to EA’s game after a long wait as it last appeared in FIFA 17. On the other hand, the Allianz Arena of Bayern Munich is not in the game because of their deal with Konami.
