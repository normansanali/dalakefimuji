Katarina Witt, the former figure skater, is now working as an ambassador and entrepreneur.  

Katarina Witt, popularly known as the East German Ice queen, left an unimaginable impression on her fans through her skill as a figure skater.

After her retirement in 2008, her fans still miss her dearly in every Olympics. As the Beijing WInter Olympics begins, netizens wonder where she has been in 2022. In the 2018 Olympics, she appeared as a TV-Olympic and skating expert for the ARD.

Skater: Where Is Katarina Witt Now In 2022?

Katarina Witt is now a sports ambassador and international show star. In addition, she is a long-term BMW cooperation partner as an ambassador for BMW-SPORT and BMW-CLASSIC, as per her website.

Likewise, she has been a “DISNEY ON ICE” ambassador and a national and worldwide spokesperson for the social lottery DPL since 2016, with George Clooney, Rafael Nadal, and Desmond Tutu.

In 2017, she co-founded “BUSINESS MEETS SPORT” alongside footballer Steffen Freund, a World Cup and Champions League champion, and entrepreneur Karsten Tornow.

She participated in her 9th Olympic Winter Games in 2018 as a TV-Olympic and skating expert for the ARD. Similarly, she has long advocated for environmental conservation, particularly the German “Blue Angel” certification for products and services.

The “Katarina Witt Foundation,” which she started in 2005, is still near her heart. Her charity strives to assist physically disabled children and teenagers.

Katarina Witt Never Got Married To Have A Husband Or Spouse
