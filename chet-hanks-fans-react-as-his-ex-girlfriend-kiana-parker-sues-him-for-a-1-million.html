Chet Hanks Fans React As His Ex-Girlfriend Kiana Parker Sues Him For A $1 Million

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Chet Hanks has been sued in a $1 million domestic abuse suit by his ex-girlfriend Kiana Parker.

Parker filed legal docs against the 30-year-old rapper/actor at the Fort Bend County Justice Center in Texas. She claims the Empire actor got physical with her on more than one occasion between October 2020 and January 2021, TMZ reported.

Parker said in court docs that Chet pushed her and grabbed her arms and wrists after she said she was leaving the room they shared at the Windsor Court Hotel.

Parker told the court that Hanks said that said she was "just a ghetto Black b****" who wouldn't be believed. Shockingly, she alleges he threatened her in a murder-suicide while they were in his Sugar Land, Texas home in November. She added that her nine-year-old twins were at home during the incident.

Social media users were left horrified last month after Chet, the son of Tom Hanks and Rita Wilson, shared a graphic video online. The clip showed Parker allegedly attacking him.

TMZ obtained video of the one time rapper in an altercation with Parker on 8 January at Hanks’s home in Texas. Despite Hanks showing a video of what appears to be Parker leaving him with a bloody forehead, Parker was reportedly granted a restraining order against him.

Chet alleges Kiana charged at him with a knife (something which she denies.) The footage apparently does show her swiping at him with a pot. Hanks recently filed a lawsuit against Parker over the alleged altercation in January. He is also suing her for theft and return of the money she allegedly stole from him.

Parker, in legal docs, said that Hanks had approached her with a knife in the incident, and she hit him with a pot to defend herself.

Yesterday, Parker shared a picture of herself on the court steps. She captioned the image:

"This is the first step on a long road to justice. Only looking forward, no looking back."
