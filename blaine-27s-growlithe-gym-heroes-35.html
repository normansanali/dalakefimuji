Blaine's Growlithe (Japanese: カツラのガーディ Katsura's Gardie) is a Fire-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Gym Heroes expansion.

Card text

Shake
ふりとばす		10
If your opponent has any Benched Pokémon, he or she chooses 1 of them and switches it with the Defending Pokémon. (Do the damage before switching the Pokémon.)
Fire Tackle
ほのおタックル		30
Blaine's Growlithe does 10 damage to itself.

Pokédex data

Release information

This card was included in the Gym Heroes expansion with artwork by Atsuko Nishida, first released in the Japanese Guren Town Gym Theme Deck. In Japan, it was reprinted as an Unnumbered Promotional card with different artwork by Ken Sugimori and was available on a two-card insert alongside Sabrina's Abra in the March 1999 issue of CoroCoro Comic, released on February 15, 1999.

Gallery

Trivia

The Japanese Unnumbered Promotional print features the Gym series symbol, indicating that it is a promotional addition to the two expansions.

Origin

Gym Leader Blaine owns a Growlithe in Pokémon Red and Blue.
