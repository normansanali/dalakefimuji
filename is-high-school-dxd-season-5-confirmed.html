Background of High School DxD

High School DxD is a Japanese light novel series written by Ichiei Ishibumi and illustrated by Miyama-Zero.

The story revolves around Issei Hyoudou, a high school student who is killed by his first date, only to be revived by a beautiful senior student, Rias Gremory, who is a devil.

The series is known for its blend of supernatural elements, action, and ecchi themes.

Previous Seasons

The anime adaptation of High School DxD has seen the release of four successful seasons. The first season aired in 2012, followed by the second season, High School DxD New, in 2013.

The third season, High School DxD BorN, aired in 2015, and the most recent season, High School DxD Hero, was released in 2018.

Following the conclusion of High School DxD Hero, fans have been eagerly awaiting news about the possibility of a fifth season.
