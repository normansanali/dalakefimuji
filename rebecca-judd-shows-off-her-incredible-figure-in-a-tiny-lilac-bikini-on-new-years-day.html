Rebecca Judd shows off her incredible figure in a tiny lilac bikini on New Year's Day

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Rebecca Judd works hard for her trim figure, putting in long hours at the gym despite having a big family and a business to run.

And the mother of four, 37, looked sensational on Friday as she flaunted her toned physique while posing for a selfie in her bedroom.

Rebecca, who is married to AFL legend Chris Judd, stripped down to a lilac bikini as she snapped a photo on her pink iPhone.

‘Let’s do this, 2021’: Footy WAG and mother-of-four Rebecca Judd showed off her incredible figure while posing in a skimpy lilac bikini on New Year’s Day 

The model looked trim and terrific in her skimpy two-piece, which was made of a ribbed material and featured high-cut briefs.

Rebecca displayed a soft golden glow, and styled her long brunette hair loosely over her shoulders.

She accessorised with a chic pair of white cat-eye sunglasses. 

Fitness secrets: Rebecca credits cardio tennis with helping her stay in shape, saying she finds running around a court with music blaring ‘really fun’

‘Feeling fresh. 2021, let’s do this,’ she captioned the post. 

Rebecca credits cardio tennis with helping her stay in shape, saying she finds running around a court with music blaring ‘really fun’.

‘It’s a cardio workout where the music blares, the coach just feeds you balls, you do awesome drills with lots of running and it’s 60 minutes that goes so quickly,’ she said in July 2019, adding that she plays for two hours, twice a week.

‘It’s the only exercise I’ve ever done in my life where I look at my watch and I go, “Damn it, there’s only 10 minutes left.” Whereas usually I when I’m exercising I’m always watching my clock wanting it to be over.’

Trim and toned: Rebecca, who is the co-founder of activewear label Jaggad, also does Pilates

Rebecca, who is the co-founder of activewear label Jaggad, also does Pilates.

The TV and radio presenter previously told Daily Mail Australia she doesn’t follow a strict diet, but always tries to have a healthy dinner.  

‘Breakfast is usually muesli and a strong latte,’ she said. ‘For lunch, I’ll grab my favourite brown rice sushi or focaccia.’

For dinner, she and Chris, 37, typically have fish or meat with vegetables.
