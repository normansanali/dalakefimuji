Meet Selling The Hamptons Mia Calabrese! Bio, Net Worth, Boyfriend, Age

Meet Mia Calabrese, star real estate agent from Discovery Plus’s new show “Selling The Hamptons”. Here you’ll learn about her net worth, boyfriend, details on her family, and her appearance on the show.

Tag along with this Mia Calabrese Bio to learn more as this article proceeds.

Mia Calabrese On Selling The Hamptons

Mia Calabrese is starring on the Discovery Plus show “Selling The Hamptons.” Although the title of the show sound similar to “Selling Sunset” both shows focuses on selling luxury real estate markets in two areas concentrated with high net-worth individuals “Selling Sunset” and “Selling The Hamptons” are not directly related to each other.

The show stands on its own and exclusively airs on Discovery Plus. The real estate agent featured on the show are part of Nest Seekers International. One of the agents featured on the show is Mia Calabrese. She makes her debut on the show with Bianca D’Alessio and Kenny Arias.

Whereas there are a few agents viewers of real estate shows are already familiar with such as J.B. Andreassi, Michael Fulfree, Peggy Zabakolas, and Jimmy Giugliano from Million Dollar Beach House.

Speaking about herself with New York Post, Mia revealed, “Anyone I know will tell you when they think of me, they think, ‘She has big d–k energy’ — that’s just who I am”. She added, “I’m going to make as much money as I humanly can — I like shiny things too.”

On 20 January 2022, before the premiere of the show, Mia revealed, “Have you guys watched the first 2 episodes?? What kind of energy did I say I have?” Back in mid-December 2021, she had announced, “ARE YOU READY? Selling the Hamptons, January 20th, 2022 @discoveryplus“.

It is to be worth noting that Mia is the only Black agent on the show.

Mia Calabrese Net Worth

Mia Calabrese built a total net worth of $1 million with her career in real estate.

Before starting her career in real estate, Mia Calabrese started as a model at the age of 16. Then after that, she became concierge for high-end nightclubs catering to Saudi Princes and tech billionaires. She worked for The Box NYC from September 2012 to September 2018 in New York as Concierge Liaison.

Her LinkedIn bio reads, “Following nearly a decade long successful career in the world of affluent hospitality Mia has naturally extended her expertise to ultra-luxury real estate servicing and fulfilling the discriminating taste of her wealthy elite clients. Her extensive experience and intuitive understanding of the art of fine living enables her to provide knowledge, access, and quality of service that exceeds expectations.”

“As proud St. John’s University alumni, Mia has earned a bachelor’s degree in Government and Political science. She is a world traveler, a wine connoisseur, and an avid cyclist. While her primary residence is in New York City, you may find her expanding and exploring her life experience and network in all corners of the globe including frequent visits to the most sought-after destinations in Europe, the Mediterranean, and South America.”

“Mia takes great pride in listening intently to clients and executing on their aspirations expeditiously and with precision. She has earned nominations for the New York City Concierge Choice awards, and is recognized across the world for her passion in service, accomplishments, and commitment to her clients and the industry.”

After that, Mia delved into real estate by joining Nest Seekers International as a licensed real estate salesperson.

Not long before the premiere of the show, Mia closed the deal of $3.8 million on the upper east side. With the level of performance that she showed, she must be making pocketing a jaw-dropping salary. According to Indeed, Nest Seekers International pays their agent nearly $139,907 per year.

Furthermore, Mia already has an IMDB profile just so you know. Also, she holds an undergraduate degree in Political Science and Government.

Who Are Mia Calabrese’s Parents And Siblings?

Mia Calabrese was born to her father Secondino ‘Sam’ Calabrese and Dawn Calabrese. In March 2016 post, Mia proclaimed that her father was her first love. Sadly, she has lost her father but is yet to be revealed when. In June 2016, on Father’s day, she posted, “Happy Fathers Day Daddy..If you knew him, you loved him. Thank you for always encouraging me to be ME. You called me your shadow because I followed you around everywhere, missing you. —baby girl”.

About her mother, Mia recently posted a video of TikTok about her mom’s reaction on her TikTok and her Instagram. She captioned, “Tune in tomorrow to Selling the Hamptons”. Mia loves her mother dearly and on mother’s day, she posted a series of pictures from a younger to how she is now on mother’s day IG posts.

Mia’s father was previously married to Karyn Calabrese-Scherer. They had a son named Secondino “Dino” Giuseppe Calabrese who died at the age of 38 in August 2008. Karyn and Secondino divorced later in their lives and Karyn married Jerrold Scherer. Mia’s other siblings and step-siblings were ClaudiaNicole (Adam) Calabrese-Rosenfelt, Nicholas Calabrese, and Michael Scherer.

Talking about her brother, Nick also works as a realtor for Compass. His professional bio reads, “Nick Calabrese is not only in the business to find his clients the right home but to make the search time efficient as well as exciting and comfortable. Born and raised in Chicago Nick has extensive knowledge of the different neighborhoods in the city. He thrives on sitting down listening to what his client’s needs are and making sure they are satisfied. His trustworthy, fun, energetic personality creates a great client-broker relationship.”

He is a member of MVP Chicago Real Estate. Mia wishes Nick his birthday on 6 August every year.
