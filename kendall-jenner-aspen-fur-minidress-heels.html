Kendall Jenner Just Wore a Fur Minidress and Heels in the Snow

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Kendall Jenner is back in Aspen—and naturally, dressing for the snowy weather in the most Kendall Jenner way ever.

The supermodel headed out for dinner Thursday night in an incredible Bottega Veneta minidress. The speckled stone-gray piece featured an asymmetrical, one-shoulder neckline covered in a fuzzy white fur lining.

Rather than cover up the outfit with a winter-appropriate coat, Jenner let the dress do most of the talking, simply walking out in sheer black tights and glossy black stiletto pumps.

BACKGRID

This isn’t the first time Jenner has pulled off a sartorial stunt on behalf of Bottega Veneta. Several times over the past few months, the 818 founder has ventured out on a casual errand (say, walking her dog or pumping gas) dressed to the nines in an ultra glamorous, head-to-toe outfit by the label. It turned out those paparazzi images were all part of a marketing campaign for the Italian fashion house’s Fall/Winter 2023 collection.

Time will tell if Jenner’s snowy dinner look was in service of yet another campaign, but this isn’t the first (and surely won’t be the last) we’ve seen of her Aspen wardrobe.

She traveled to the luxe Colorado ski town last month to host a FWRD pop-up shop. And while grabbing dinner with pal Hailey Bieber soon after, Jenner stepped out wearing a more seasonally appropriate look—specifically, she bundled up in a long ivory fur coat layered over a black turtleneck, black pants, and black square-toe leather boots.
