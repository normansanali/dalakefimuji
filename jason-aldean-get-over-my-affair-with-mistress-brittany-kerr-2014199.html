Jason Aldean: &quot;Get Over&quot; My Affair With Mistress Brittany Kerr

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Breakin’ it down! Jason Aldean told Billboard magazine (very adamantly!) in its Sept. 27 issue that he would like people to move on from his affair with Brittany Kerr.

“It has been two years of this s—,” Aldean, 37, said of his cheating scandal. “Get over it, already! And then when I finally do get enough of it and say something, every newspaper grabs it. And I’m not trying to get in the headlines — I’m just trying to get people to stop running their mouths.”

The country megastar was caught kissing American Idol contestant Brittany Kerr in a Hollywood bar back in September 2012, while he was still married to his wife Jessica Ussery. (The ex spouses of 12 years share two little girls, Keeley, 11, and Kendyl, 6.) Aldean apologized for his indiscretion and attempted to repair his marriage, only to file for divorce in 2013.

This past June, Kerr controversially accompanied Aldean to the 2014 CMT Awards in Nashville. One month later, Aldean took to his Instagram to defend his relationship with his former mistress. “So sick of people judging me and @brittanylkerr over things they know nothing about,” he wrote. “I have made mistakes but i am a better person because of it, and wouldnt change a thing. Im happier than i have ever been, so thank u to all the people who support us. We are happy and life goes on. Its time to move on people.”

Not much has changed since his public plea, as Aldean admitted to Billboard that the negative feedback still eats away at him. “I’m pretty private, but over the last couple of years I’ve gotten that taken away from me, which kind of pissed me off,” he said. “You know, my kids get on there and read all that. I’m not blaming anybody. Obviously, a lot of that stuff is my own deal. But I could go post something on my page right now that says, ‘Hey, we just donated $5 million to a children’s cancer research center,’ and somebody would get on there and go, ‘You’re an asshole cheater.'”
